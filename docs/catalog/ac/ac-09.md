# AC-9 Previous Logon Notification {#ac-09}

_**Implementation Level**_: System

### Control

Notify the user, upon successful logon to the system, of the date and time of the last logon.

<details>
  <summary>Supplemental Guidance</summary>

Notify the user, upon successful logon to the system, of the date and time of the last logon.

</details>
